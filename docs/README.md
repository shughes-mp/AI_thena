# AI_thena Product And Implementation Documents

## Phase 0: Baseline

[Phase 0](phase-0/README.md) records the current architecture, terminology gaps, recommendation traceability, test baseline, fixtures, and risks.

## Phase 1: Contracts

[Phase 1](phase-1/README.md) defines the binding product, terminology, evidence, facilitation, governance, and versioning contracts for later implementation.

## Phase 2: Evidence Provenance And Review

[Phase 2](phase-2/README.md) implements traceable evidence signals, instructor review, authorization, and learner capabilities.

## Phase 3: Source Grounding And Assessment Protection

[Phase 3](phase-3/README.md) implements passage-grounded tutor responses, unsupported-source handling, protected-answer interception, and instructor audit records.

[Phase 4](phase-4/README.md) strengthens learner orientation, proportional help, purpose-aware dialogue, formative summaries, and learner-authored reflection or correction.

[Phase 0–4 hardening audit](phase-4/hardening-audit.md) records the security, dependency, CI, resilience, abuse-control, deletion, and verification work completed before Phase 5.

## Phase 6: Teaching Briefs And Evidence Maps

[Phase 6](phase-6/README.md) implements versioned teaching briefs, purpose-specific evidence maps, review-aware evidence drill-down, and provenance-rich PDF export.

## Phase 5: Instructor Evidence Review Surfaces

[Phase 5](phase-5/README.md) implements complete signal inspection, filtering, review history, live uncertainty explanations, learning-outcome context, and source-linked misunderstanding review.

## Phase 7: Facilitation Intelligence

[Phase 7](phase-7/README.md) implements deployed and production-verified deterministic Observer, Guide, and Conductor recommendations, live and report integration, explicit release conditions, and instructor-controlled review and outcome recording.

## Phase 8: Planning, Preview, And Anticipated Pivots

[Phase 8](phase-8/README.md) implements a faithful learner-experience preview, deterministic session-design checks, participation planning, and editable Guide-first anticipated pivots before learners begin.

## Phase 8.5: Instructor UX Coherence And Progressive Disclosure

[Phase 8.5](phase-8-5/README.md) reframes the instructor workflow around before/during/after learner work, separates required setup from recommended preparation and optional audit details, gates sharing behind a saved learner preview, adds recall-only question cautions, and preserves advanced evidence/source-use inspection behind progressive disclosure.
